About Me

My name is David and I am in love with my mountain bike. I'll begin this blog post with less about me, and more about my bike.

No matter if you're just starting out or have been crushing technical lines for years the Devinci Troy S Complete Mountain Bike is great for everyone.

Spec'd with RockShox suspension components and an adjustable Split-Pivot linkage is plenty stout enough to handle jumps and drops while still remaining efficient on climbs.

A dual front chain ring with 11-speed rear cassette give plenty of gearing for just about any situation.

With the versatile geometry of the Devinci Troy S Complete Mountain Bike you'll have a fun, flexible ride that won't let you down when the trail gets spicy.
